Our Poultry Software Solutions

Whether you are an integrated, semi- integrated or horizontally integrated poultry company, our software can help you improve your business results. With our comprehensive functionality and user-friendly interface, you can accurately plan your production, manage inventory, perform quality checks and more.

Dashboard Farm Monitor

You will receive the Farm Monitor with all these products. This is a customised product that provides a good overview through use of a dashboard. Comprehensive results pages and summaries of the daily status are standard features of the Farm Monitor. This allows your production manager, the general management or selected ‘third parties’ to monitor, check and manage the various production processes. In addition, the Farm Monitor offers the option to send alerts to your smartphone or email in case of emergency.

Our solutions

OptiRearing

You can track your hens' performance by entering key data into the system, which also pulls information from your parent stock. it increase easy-to-read benchmarks,so you can quickly see how your flock is doing compared to previous ones and other farms.A personalized dashboard gives you a clear overview Of all the important numbers to helo you make quick decisions.

OptiBreed

by entering your breeding farm's production result into the system, you can easily compare your parformance with previous flock and other parrent stock farms. This provides valuable insights for improvement, helping you make informed desition. With this information, you can optimize your breeding paractices for better outcome

OptiHatch

Hatchery employees log key data such as initial figures, Hatching results, and quality paramiters. QR codes are used to ensure full traceability of every batch of eggs, making tracking effortless. With the hatching egg planning feature, you can create accurate short-term forecasts, allowing you to uptimize your operations effectively.

OptiLayers

With poultryplan, you can easily monitor laying result and access key data from the poultry-rearing farm. This information can be directly linked to the egg packing station, using tool like QR code for seamless tracking .Egg quality data and full traceability are also integrated into the system, providing a completing solution for managing your egg production process.

OptiBroilers

As a broilers farmer, you'll get real-time updates from the hatchery directly through our poultry farm management software. With poultryplan, you can easily log your production results and instantly compare them with other farmers. The clear benchmark overview helps you see where you stand and make quick adjustments to improve your flock's performance.

OptiSlaughter

With poultryplan, you can benchmark and trace processing plant result for each batch. you'll be able to measure and view the performance of flocks and barns in detail. Thanks to it integrated design, poultryplan allows you to analyze data throughout the entire production chain-from farm to procsssing plant, all the way to retail.

OptiFeed

Wondering when to deliver feed to site X or customer Y, and which silo to use? poultryplan provideds smart suggestions on the type and amount of feed to supply, all based on real-time flock data and feed schedules you can also compare these suhggestions to past deliveries and performance for even better decision-making.
